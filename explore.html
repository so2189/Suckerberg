Meta Horizon includes destinations like Horizon Worlds, Horizon Workrooms and your Horizon Home. You’ll show up as your Meta Horizon profile in across all VR apps so we can track your data across the entire metaverse. The more trapped you are, the more money we make!

High Praise

Our Horizon Worlds has been described as "surprisingly decent on the web and mobile," revealing how much customers enjoy playing in our world.

Unique Graphics

In Horizon Worlds, you and your friends can collaborate and bring your wildest ideas to life—without ever leaving VR. Whether you are looking for community, games, events or everything in between, this is the place to be.

Selective Access

Unlike earlier metaverse worlds like Second Life, which allows users to join for free, Horizon Worlds on VR is exclusively for people who can afford our $300-$1,500 VR headsets! This restricts Horizon Worlds to a close knit community of the upper class.

Why We're Better

Second Life provided its users with transparency about their data and privacy policies, but we have yet to define our data practices so you won’t need to worry about reading boring privacy policies.

Accuracy

Our Quest Pro headsets have 16 cameras which allow us to have even more data points to work with for accuracy. When you use the Quest Pro, Meta will know even more about your surroundings, not to mention your facial expressions and eye movements. We will know and see everything.
